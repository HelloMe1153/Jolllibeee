About Us

Our History

In order to meet the needs of its growing Filipino population, Jollibee opened restaurants abroad in the 1990s and 2000s, customizing its menu to suit regional preferences while keeping its essential items. In addition to being a well-known national symbol in the Philippines, Jollibee is becoming a more well-known brand throughout Asia, the Middle East, North America, and Europe. With its goal of bringing happiness to consumers all around the world through delicious food and welcoming, family-friendly service, Jollibee keeps innovating and expanding.

Our Mission

To serve great tasting food, bringing the joy of eating to everyone.

Why Choose Us

Jollibee is known for its unique menu offerings that cater to the Filipino palate, friendly customer service, and community involvement.

Community Initiatives

Jollibee actively participates in various community programs, including feeding programs, disaster response efforts, and youth development projects. We believe in giving back to the communities we serve.
